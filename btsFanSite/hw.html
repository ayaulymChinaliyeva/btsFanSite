General information

BTS (eng. bulletproof boyscouts) - is a korean pop idol group.

They debuted on 13th of June, 2013. Members :

leader - Kim Namjoon (Rap Monster);
visual - Kim Seokjin (Jin);
lead rapper, composer - Min Yungi (Suga);
lead dancer - Jung Hoseok (J-Hope);
lead dancer, vocalist - Park Jimin (Jimin);
dancer, vocalist - Kim Taehyung (V);
frontman, lead vocalist - Jeon Jungkook (Jungkook);
